Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 21, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you register, we collect your name, email address, university affiliation, major, skills, and bio. If you register with an institutional (.edu) email, we verify your university membership.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to operate and improve useArco, match you with relevant projects and collaborators, send transactional emails, and provide support. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Authentication and Cookies

useArco uses JSON Web Tokens (JWT) stored in browser cookies to maintain your session. These cookies expire after 30 days and can be cleared via your browser settings at any time.

4. Data Sharing

Your profile (name, university, skills, bio) is visible to other verified users. Your email is never publicly displayed. We may share anonymized data with university partners for research purposes.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data while your account is active. You may request deletion by emailing hello@usearco.com.

6. Security

We use HTTPS encryption, bcrypt password hashing, rate limiting, and security headers. No method of internet transmission is 100% secure.

7. Contact

For privacy questions contact us at hello@usearco.com.
