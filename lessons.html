The $0 Business Lesson

HOOK:
"Stop trying to validate your business idea. Here's what actually works."

BODY:
Most founders spend 6 months building something nobody asked for. The move?
Sell it before you build it.

Pick up the phone. Call 10 people who would be your customer.
Tell them exactly what you're building and ask them to pay you right now — even just $50.

If nobody pays, you don't have a business. You have a hobby.
If 3 people pay? You just validated your idea in 48 hours — not 6 months.

CALL TO ACTION:
This is called pre-selling. It's how the smartest founders de-risk everything.

Project Management Relatable Pain Point ~50 sec

Why Your To-Do List Is Lying to You

HOOK:
"If you end every day feeling busy but accomplished nothing — this is why."

BODY:
Your to-do list has 20 items. You knock out 18 of them and still feel like you failed.

That's because you're confusing activity with progress.

Here's the fix: Every morning, write down ONE task — the single thing that, if done, makes everything else easier or irrelevant.

Gary Keller calls it the One Thing. I call it your $10,000 task.

Do that first. Everything else is noise.

CALL TO ACTION:
The people who build real businesses protect their most important work fiercely.
